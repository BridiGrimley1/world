Welcome to United Arab Emriates

A captivating destination where ancient traditions meet modern marvels. The United Arab Emirates (UAE) is a country located in the Middle East. It borders Oman, the Persian Gulf and Saudi Arabia. Dubai and Abu Dhabi, the capital of the UAE, are the two best-known cities in the country.

About United Arab Emriates

  • Capital City: Abu Dhabi
  • Population: The current population of United Arab Emriates is 9,264,000
  • Climate: The United Arab Emirates has a desert climate with hot and dry conditions.
  • Due to the country's location on the Arabian Peninsula, scorching summers prevail with average temperatures between 35 and 45 °C. Coastal areas, including Dubai and Abu Dhabi, are influenced by the Persian Gulf, resulting in slightly higher humidity with fresher winds.
  • Winters are generally mild and pleasant, with average temperatures between 15 °C and 25 °C. Inland, nights are considerably cooler during this time of year.
